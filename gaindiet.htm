Gaining Muscle

Main idea is to eat as much as possible while keeping an eye on fats.
Keep calorie intake high especially if metabolism is fast. The harder it is
for you to gain weight, the more you should eat. When working out, avoid cardio
and lift heavy with a good amount of rest in between so that you can give every set your all.
